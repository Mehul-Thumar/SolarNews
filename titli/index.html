THE AIM

We envisioned Butterfly in my Backyard TitliTyar or Butterfly Festival with an aim to develop awareness of these precious creations amongst the local community at Corbett and the scores of tourists that visit every year.To be organised every year in the autumnal conjunction of the rainy season and the impending winters, TitliTyar is a celebration of all that is beautiful and bountiful in our world. In continuation of our efforts from the Lockdown Year 2020, the organisers visualize a wider scope for this years’ event and would like to involve more stakeholders to be a part of this annual butterfly extravaganza.

THE LOCATION

The principal location for this years’ TitliTyar is the biodiversity hotspot of Kyari in the Corbett Landscape region. An amazing countryside coupled with an almost complete ecosystem, Kyari is a gem in the Corbett National Park area with an array of vegetation supporting a range of smaller species at the base of the food chain. In the heart of Kyari, Alaya Resort is the primary host for the event, following up on a successful 2020 event. Other areas that will be covered for TitliTyar:
Corbett Belt – Dhikuli, Ringora, Kumeriya, Dhela, Sawaldeh | Nainital Belt – Pangot

THE IDEA

TitliTyar 2021 will spread awareness on the sensitive topic of Habitat Creation for Butterflies in and around the Corbett Landscape. Our attempt is to involve the local community of Ramnagar as well as the professional individuals from various trades working in the area. The idea is to germinate the minds of every age group and from every sphere of life with the beauty of these fairy like winged angels and their importance in our ecosystem. Various interactive sessions and experiential activities will be held over the 2 week duration of TitliTyar 2021 with subject experts to guide those attending from the local community, as well as the tourists. Our endeavor is to extend the scope of the festival to every butterfly hotspot across the state and country in the coming years and hold such knowledge sessions twice a year.

THE OBJECTIVE

Being connected to Mother Nature and its glorious creatures is extremely important in today’s scenario of tremendous climate change and environment instability. It has become extremely important for the tourism industry to be the forerunner in preservation of our natural biodiversity, and in conservation of the smaller species of the food chain. TitliTyar is our attempt to focus on the beautiful and bountiful ecology of butterflies, and this festival will interest the traveling tourist as well as involve the local community as equal stakeholders in protecting our world.Through this festival, we share the world of butterfly knowledge and experience that we attempt to create and curate on an annual basis, initially beginning in the forests of Corbett and expanding to other butterfly hotspots across the country.

Tyar Foundation

Tyar Foundation is imagined as the groundwork of an intensive country wide social awakening campaign centering at the idea of Citizen Contribution on issues directly impacting their day to day lives, and as a celebration of nature playing an intrinsic part of their world. TitliTyar is our effort, using butterflies, at a local level to bring about a small behavioral change in the perception of communities towards nature, which may lead to a quiet revolution and add up to significant national gains for the environment.

We look forward to your wherewithal and hope for a long association for the Butterflies – TitliTyar.
