JIM CORBETT

Corbett National Park - A truly wild experience

Description

Romantic walks, adrenaline high, soul search, an educational trip, a few cherishing moments with family or a perfect me-time - no matter the reason, Jim Corbett is the perfect choice for all!

Setting the bar pretty high for a weekend getaway, especially from nearby cities like Delhi, Jim Corbett guarantees its visitors a real made-for-you vacation, which fits perfectly according to the needs of the tourists. The deep rooted nature makes it possible to reconnect with Mother Earth and absorb her serenity and peace. The rich biodiversity provides support to many educational trips to this land too. On the other hand, the adventurous activities that you can take part in at Jim Corbett are numerous - river rafting, rope slithering, body surfing, mountain biking, and the list goes on. The great Kumaoni tradition is no less pure and attractive - live and cherish the culture of the land through food, dances, monuments and shopping.

Above all, see the Royal Tigers in all their might LIVE. Be a witness to even a tiger catching its prey! Or see them walk around casually, taking tours like you.
